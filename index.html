About

Hi, I'm Sanika Pradhan, a scientist by training and an artist by heart—two worlds that collide beautifully here. My journey weaves through microscopes and canvas, where science reveals the mechanisms of the universe, and art reimagines them with color, emotion, and wonder. At The SciArt Studio, I illustrate the elegance of scientific disciplines, from cells to galaxies—with a splash of creative energy. This space is where formulas find rhythm, molecules find movement, and complexity finds beauty. Science fuels the logic. Art sparks the magic. And together, they energize everything I create.

Services Offered

  • Creative Collaborations
    Whether you're an artist curious about science or a scientist craving creativity, The SciArt Studio is your bridge. Let’s team up to bring your ideas to life—from concept to canvas.
  • Publication-Ready Scientific Diagrams
    Need clear, beautiful visuals for your research? I create formal, high-quality illustrations—perfect for publications, presentations, or posters.
  • Sci-Inspired Digital Merchandise
    Science gets stylish here! Explore digital artwork as prints, postcards, bookmarks, and more—where molecules meet magic.
  • Custom Artwork Commissions
    Celebrate milestones like PhDs, graduations, or breakthroughs with personalized art.
  • 1:1 Sci-Communication Sessions
    I help scientists visually reimagine their work for engaging science communication.
